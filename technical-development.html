Frequently Asked Questions

What are technology services and software development?

Technology services help businesses use digital tools to operate businesses efficiently. Software development is an important part that creates custom applications, web platforms, mobile apps, and enterprise solutions that correspond to business requirements.

How to choose the right software development service provider?

Look for a provider with industry experience, a proven record, and expertise in technologies like Java, Python, ERP systems, and agile practices.

What technology is used for custom software development?

Custom software often uses Java or Python for back-end, React or Angular for front-end, ERPNext and Odoo for ERP solutions, and Databricks for data analytics, with a cloud platform for scalability.

How do ERP systems make business operations easier?

ERP systems such as ERPNext and Odoo centralize data, automate workflows, provide real-time insights, reduce manual work, and improve efficiency in departments.

How can web and app development improve customer engagement?

Websites and apps create seamless, interactive experiences that connect with users, build trust, encourage repeat interactions, and boost conversions and loyalty.
