What is TNova ERP?

TNova ERP is an integrated enterprise resource management software that meets the business requirements of a company in one single place. By a merge of ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning), CRM (Customer Relationship Management), and HRM (Human Resource Management), the system harmonizes the workflows, upgrades the efficiency, and data becomes the foundation of the decisions.

It is a single platform that gives you the power to control any business aspect, such as finance, inventory, and sales, alongside HR and production by simply automating the workflows, getting insights into your operations, and removing the redundancies.

The customizable feature makes us stand out from the crowd to give businesses the freedom to personalize the system to their specific processes and the needs that are constantly changing, ensuring a perfect fit that leads to increased turnover and enhanced productivity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ERP software and what does it do?
ERP software, also known as Enterprise Resource Planning, is a comprehensive suite of applications that are interconnected and serve the purpose of managing and planning the business operations for the organizations on the basis of various departments like finance, inventory, production, human resources, and customer relationships.
What are the benefits of using ERP software?
The productivity resulting from ERP software is quite significant, facilitating the merging of resources and data across the different departments, enhancing the decision-making process, standardization of the organization's processes, compliance, and the provision of precise financial reporting.
What are the different types of ERP solutions?
ERP solutions can be classified into different types based on deployment, including on-premise, cloud-based, hybrid, open-source, and industry-specific ERP systems. All these options vary in cost, customization, and scalability.
How does ERP software support multiple locations and remote access?
Cloud-based ERP systems empower employees at different locations to have access to the system from a distance, thereby allowing an efficient workflow and implementation of the same procedure at the branches or in the different locations.
What are the common challenges in ERP implementation?
Common challenges that arise during ERP implementation are not having clearly defined requirements, user resistance towards the new system, problems in migrating data, and a lack of support over time. The most successful ERP providers tackle these issues by providing customized solutions, user training, top-notch migration tools, and expert support.
