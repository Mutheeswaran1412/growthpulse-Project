What is Growthpulse LIMS?

Growthpulse LIMS is an AI-integrated, web-based Laboratory Information Management System. We provide industry-specific regulatory compliance for managing laboratory data and operations. Our LIMS is designed for global access and deployed either on-premises or in the cloud, supporting both no-code and low-code customization for ease of configuration and integration.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is LIMS?
A laboratory information management system (LIMS) is a computer program that works by improving accuracy and compliance, helping a laboratory efficiently handle the samples, the data, and the workflow.
What is the process of LIMS?
LIMS brings down the operation of a laboratory through the tracking of samples, data collection, analysis, and reporting, and often integrates with the laboratory equipment to automate the entire working process.
What software is used in a laboratory?
Some of the most common software used in laboratories are LIMS, electronic laboratory notebook (ELN), laboratory execution system (LES), and specialized analysis tools for overall management.
What are LIMS licensing and deployment models?
LIMS can be installed on-site for complete control or using cloud-based SaaS for various laboratory requirements that demand flexibility and low-maintenance options.
What are the different types of LIMS?
LIMS can be classified into commercial off-the-shelf, customized point solutions, and systems developed in-house according to specific workflows.
